## Why a local AI consultant rather than a Paris agency?

### Local knowledge changes everything

An AI consultant based in the Tarn department understands the local business landscape: the constraints of construction tradespeople managing 5 to 15 job sites simultaneously, the billing cycles of Tarn-based B2B providers, the payment delays from local authorities. A Paris agency treats your project as one ticket among 200. A local expert understands that your 8-employee plumbing company in Carmaux doesn't have the same needs as a 50-person Parisian startup.

Geographic proximity enables in-person meetings. An on-site audit reveals problems invisible over video calls: the paper order book, the Excel spreadsheet shared via USB drive, the quotes sent from a personal phone. These details shape the technical solution.

### Significantly lower costs

No Paris-Albi travel expenses (€250-400 per trip for train + hotel). No inflated Parisian day rates driven by office rent in the 9th arrondissement. A local freelancer charges €800/day compared to €1,500 to €3,000/day for a Paris agency. On a 10-day project, the difference is €7,000 to €22,000.

### Access to regional grants that no one tells you about

A local AI consultant masters the funding programs available to Tarn businesses:

- **OCCAL (Occitanie)**: grant covering up to 70% of digital transformation expenses, capped at €23,000. Eligible for automation projects, chatbots, dashboards.
- **Pass Occitanie**: regional aid covering 50% of digital investments, capped at €10,000. Available to SMBs with fewer than 50 employees.
- **OPCO**: covers training costs for using the deployed AI tools. The amount depends on the industry branch and number of employees.

A €2,900 project can come down to €870 after the OCCAL grant. A local consultant knows the forms, the deadlines, the contacts at the Tarn Chamber of Commerce and the Occitanie Region.

## How to choose your AI consultant in the Tarn department

### The criteria that matter

- **Verifiable portfolio**: delivered projects with measurable results, not Figma mockups
- **Transparent pricing**: rate card published publicly, no "price on request" hiding opaque margins
- **Real technical expertise**: the consultant codes themselves or directly supervises the technical work, not outsourced to an offshore developer
- **Local availability**: meeting possible within the week, not in 3 months

### Red flags to watch for

- No professional website (a digital consultant without a website is a paradox)
- No delivered projects visible online
- "Price on request only" with no reference rate card
- Result promises without verifiable metrics ("we'll boost your business")
- Technical jargon without clear explanation ("we'll deploy a fine-tuned LLM on your data" without explaining what it changes for you)

### 5 questions to ask before signing

1. Can you show me 3 projects delivered in the last 6 months?
2. What is the guaranteed delivery timeline?
3. What happens if I'm not satisfied with the result?
4. Are you familiar with regional grants (OCCAL, Pass Occitanie, OPCO) and do you help prepare the application?
5. Who will code the project — you or a subcontractor?

## FAQ

### Can an AI consultant help me if I have zero technical skills?

Yes. That's precisely the consultant's role: translate your business needs into technical solutions, deploy them, and train you to use them. The system is delivered turnkey. The included training (1 to 2 hours) covers everything you need to know. You don't need to know how to code to use a chatbot or consult a dashboard.

### Do OPCO grants cover AI automation projects?

OPCOs fund training, not the tool itself. In practice, the "training on using the deployed AI" component is OPCO-eligible. For the technical development, OCCAL and Pass Occitanie grants are better suited. A good consultant prepares both applications in parallel to maximize coverage.

### What's the difference between an AI consultant and a web developer?

A web developer builds websites and applications. An AI consultant analyzes your business processes, identifies automatable tasks, designs the solution, develops it and measures the ROI. The AI consultant has dual expertise: technical (code, APIs, integrations) and strategic (business diagnostic, profitability calculation, project prioritization). A web developer won't tell you that your real problem is quote follow-up, not a website redesign.

### How long until I see the first results?

Between 5 and 10 business days for a standard project (automation + chatbot). The first time savings are visible from the first week of use. Full ROI is measured over the first month.
