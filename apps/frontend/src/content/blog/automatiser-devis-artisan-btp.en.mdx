A construction tradesperson loses an average of 3 to 5 hours per week writing, sending and following up on quotes manually. With a simple automation system, that time drops to 30 minutes. Here's exactly how to do it.

## How much time does a tradesperson lose on quotes?

A tradesperson with 5 to 10 employees handles an average of 8 to 15 quote requests per week. Each quote takes 20 to 40 minutes: assessing the need, calculating quantities, writing, formatting, sending by email. Not to mention follow-ups — 80% of quotes require at least one follow-up to get signed.

**Real calculation:**
- 10 quotes/week × 30 min = 5h/week
- 5h × €45/h (tradesperson hourly cost) = €225/week of lost value
- Over a year: **€11,700 of unbilled time**

## The 3 steps to automate your quotes

### Step 1: The smart request form

Instead of taking requests by phone and email, an online form automatically qualifies the need. The client selects the type of work, the surface area, the urgency level. The system generates a real-time estimate.

**Recommended tools:** Typeform (free up to 10 responses/month), Tally (free unlimited), or a custom form built into the website.

### Step 2: Automatic quote generation

From the form responses, an n8n or Make workflow generates the quote in PDF format with pre-configured rates, the company logo and legal notices. The quote is automatically sent to the client by email.

**Time saved:** from 30 minutes to 5 minutes per quote.

### Step 3: Automatic follow-ups

An automatic email sequence follows up with the client at D+3, D+7 and D+14 if the quote isn't signed. Each email is personalized with the project name and amount.

**Impact:** the quote conversion rate goes from 25% to 40% on average thanks to systematic follow-ups.

## FAQ

### Can a tradesperson with no technical skills use this system?

Yes. The system is delivered turnkey and works without technical intervention. The included training (1h) is enough to be autonomous.

### Do I need a website to automate my quotes?

No, but it's recommended. A form can be hosted on a dedicated page (landing page) without a full website. The cost of a dedicated landing page is €500 to €1,000.

### Do financial aid programs cover this type of project?

Yes. OCCAL (Occitanie region) covers up to 70% of digitalization expenses for tradespeople, with a cap of €23,000. The Pass Occitanie covers 50% (cap €10,000).
